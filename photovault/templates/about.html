PhotoVault - Professional Photo Management Platform

PhotoVault is a comprehensive photo management platform designed for secure storage, advanced editing, and intelligent organization of photographs. Built by Calmic Sdn Bhd, it combines enterprise-grade security with user-friendly features.

Core Photo Management
  • Secure Upload & Storage: Upload photos from files or capture directly with integrated camera
  • Dual Version Management: Maintains both original and edited versions of every photo
  • Comprehensive Metadata: Automatically extracts and stores EXIF data including camera settings, GPS coordinates, and timestamps
  • Thumbnail Generation: Creates optimized thumbnails for fast browsing
  • Multi-format Support: Handles various image formats (PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, BMP, WebP)
Intelligent Face Detection & Recognition
  • Automatic Face Detection: Uses OpenCV computer vision to automatically detect faces in uploaded photos
  • Bounding Box Mapping: Stores precise face locations with confidence scores
  • Person Assignment: Link detected faces to people in your contact database
  • Smart Tagging: Combines automatic detection with manual tagging capabilities
